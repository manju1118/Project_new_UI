Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to CodeMaster! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our platform.

Data Collection

We collect personal data when you register on our platform, subscribe to our newsletter, or participate in coding challenges. The types of data we may collect include:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Account Usage Information
  • IP Address

We use this data to improve our platform, communicate with you, and personalize your experience.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and provide relevant content. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Data Protection

Your personal data is protected by industry-standard security measures. We implement appropriate physical, electronic, and administrative safeguards to ensure that your data is secure and protected from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal data with third parties except in the following cases:

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights or the safety of our users
  • With service providers who assist us in operating our platform

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections to your data
  • Request the deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the contact information provided below.
